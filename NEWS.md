# aardl 1.0.0

* Initial CRAN release.
* Implements the Augmented ARDL (A-ARDL) cointegration framework of
  Sam, McNown and Goh (2019).
* Eight model variants: aardl, baardl, faardl, fbaardl, nardl, fanardl,
  banardl, fbanardl.
* Three-test cointegration framework resolving degenerate cases.
* Fourier frequency selection following Yilanci et al. (2020).
* Bootstrap inference following McNown et al. (2018) and Bertelli et al. (2022).
* NARDL decomposition following Shin, Yu and Greenwood-Nimmo (2014).
